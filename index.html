Abbey Road Services

Challenge

Abbey Road wanted a simple platform to advertise their cleaning services and to connect to potential customers. It was important that the website be easy to maintain and update with new information, as well as connect with their target audience through social media.

Solution

After meeting with the client, we determined that a landing page would most closely fit her needs. I designed and developed a single page layout and developed it into a custom WordPress theme. I developed a social media presence for the client and implemented basic SEO.

Project Details

  • Landing Page
  • Wordpress Theme
  • Social Media and SEO

Excellens Photography

Challenge

Excellens came to me looking for a platform that would allow them to showcase their as well as connect them with potential clients. It was essential that the platform contain a blog feature, but also a section to showcase selected work. The client desired something simple and functional, but did not wish to sacrifice design quality.

Solution

I took a minimalist design approach for this project. With plenty of visual content, it was important for the design to accentuate, but not distract, from the photographs.

The end result was a custom WordPress theme with a minimalist feel.

Project Details

  • Minimalist Design
  • Wordpress Theme
  • Social Media and SEO

FurFocus

Challenge

Furfocus approached me without any web presence at all. They had already hired a designer and were looking for someone to develop the site based on mock-ups.

Solution

I coded their website based on the mock-ups provided, purchased a domain name on their behalf, set up their web host and created social media accounts for them. I also set up a Google Analytics account for them.

Project Details

  • Front-end Development
  • Hosting Set-up
  • Social Media and Google Analytics
