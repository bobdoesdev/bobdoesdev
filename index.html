How It Works

HVAC expenses are one of the largest expenses in the hospitality industry. The large capital expenditure needed for current energy-saving products, coupled with their meager savings, makes HVAC efficiency a very low priority for most hotel operators.

So the U.S. remains a nation full of hotel rooms that continue to waste energy. ESGNA has developed an innovative solution to this problem. With an actively managed, wireless, non-thermostat, custom-made, energy-management system, hotel operators can leave the management of individual in-room temperatures to our remote-access team of professionals.

about us

We are a United States based company who, along with our partners, have accumulated experience of dozens of years in the hospitality sector. We specialize in temperature and comfort automation in hotel guestrooms. Our innovative system produces a significant savings in energy consumption while maintaining and / or increasing guest comfort through temperature control.

With our partners we design, produce, install and monitor the only energy savings system that seamlessly integrates with existing HVAC systems and provides a pleasant temperature while reducing energy consumption and increasing the bottom line of the hotel.
